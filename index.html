A AR Restaurant App

For my module special applications of computer science: Augmented Reality applications and theoretical foundations I programmed in a team of 3, a AR App for restaurants. The Goal was to render a menu card in restaurants in a AR enviroment, to show you a 3D model of your chosen food, before ordering.

A 2D Pixel Game

In my module game engines I programmed in a group of 4 people, to build a 2D Zelda-like game. The goal is to move around the map and destroy all pots, while a timer runs out and enemies are chasing you.

A 3D Low Poly Game

The secound project in my game engines modul was a 3D game. We created again in a group of 4 people a overcooked-inspired, cooking game with a low poly graphic. You can earn score points and play with up to 4 players in local coop.
